Michael Milroy

Agile Coach and Software Developer

Michael is a Marine Corps Veteran with strong expertise in signals intelligence collection, analysis, and reporting. He received his bachelor's degree from Purdue University in 2018 and joined the Indiana State Government right out of college. Michael has since worked as a Project Manager and Agile Coach while filling the roles of Development Team Member, Scrum Master, and led an Agile transformation for the Indiana Bureau of Motor Vehicles in 2019. Michael is now moving into software development full time in hopes of combining his love for data and statistics with his developed programming skills.

Michael enjoys spending time with his wife and children, golfing with his brothers, and watching all things television regarding life in the 1800's. He also spends his daily commute time listening to audiobooks from various industries to enhance his foundational knowledge of business.
